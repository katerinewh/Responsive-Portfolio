About Me

Hello! My name is Katerine Whattoff-Hall and I am a full-stack web developer.

I’ve always sought out opportunities and challenges that are meaningful to me. Although my professional path has taken many twists and turns — from radio and television broadcasting, to fundraising for non-profit organizations, to higher education executive leadership — I've never stopped engaging my passion to help others and solve problems. As a web developer, I enjoy using my obsessive attention to detail, my unequivocal love for making things, and my mission-driven work ethic to literally change the world. That's why I’m excited to make a big impact creating software for clients ranging from individuals and small businesses all the way to large enterprise corporations...I want to make things that make a difference.
